From Zero to Web Hero

A journal of reflections from the journey through JavaScript.





What on Earth?

After four weeks of ploughing through the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, I felt the need to step back, gather my thoughts and organise them into some kind of journal. I wanted the satisfaction that comes from physical evidence of progress being made, so I set myself the challenge of creating this basic site. I took inspiration from a mixture of simple blogs like this one, websites like these and my own imagination. This website is an expression of putting theory into practice while documenting experiences, thoughts and ideas that emerge from the journey through JavaScript. I've tried to apply as much of what I've learned so far as possible. Here goes.





Who on Earth?

A wandering minstrel / linguist. I currently produce and perform a brand of #deepstring music (click the violin logo on the right to learn more) and develop EFL (English as a Foreign Language) resources such as courses and online tests. I see front end web development as a way of fulfilling my ambition of providing future generations with tools that will help them enjoy the same opportunities my privileged upbringing afforded me.





Any Plans?

After this opening piece, I'd like to start building up a portfolio of work while continuing to train myself in the art of JavaScript. As well as journal entries, I'd like to have a go at building more complex sites, starting with a remake of this one I made a while ago using WordPress. In the long term I'd like to get into building educational apps for ipad that make learning foreign languages and music more accessible to early learners (children under the age of 7). I hear this is a good place to start!
